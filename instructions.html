Overview

The user study will consist of the following steps. The times below are estimates; however, you may take as much time as you like.

  • Background questionnaire (5 minutes)
  • Demonstration of the interface (5 minutes)
  • Study task (15-20 minutes)
  • Post-study questionnaire and compensation form (5 minutes)
Note that you must enter your information in the final form to be compensated for your participation in the user study. If you opt out of the study before reaching the final form, contact Emily Wall (emilywall@gatech.edu) regarding compensation.

Task

You will use the provided interactive visualization tool to explore a dataset of NBA basketball players. Your task in this study is to use the data provided to assign each player to the position you believe they play.
